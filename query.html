Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How can I obtain a product catalog?
A: Our complete product catalog is available by postal mail. Please use the form above to request a copy, indicating "Product Catalog Request" in the inquiry details.

Q: Do you offer international shipping?
A: Yes, Hisen Corporation ships to over 50 countries worldwide. Please specify your shipping destination in the inquiry form for a detailed quote.

Q: What is the typical lead time for custom orders?
A: Lead times vary significantly based on product complexity and current manufacturing load. For an accurate estimate, please submit a detailed inquiry outlining your custom requirements.

Q: Is technical support available for legacy products?
A: Hisen is committed to supporting our products for their full lifecycle. Limited technical documentation and spare parts may be available for older models. Please submit an inquiry with your product model number for assistance.
