Our Global Corporate Facilities

Hisen Corporation operates multiple high-security engineering facilities designed for rapid fabrication, materials testing, and regional logistics control.

  • Hisen Foundry East (Allentown, PA): Our flagship assembly and metal forging foundry. Servicing the heavy industrial market since 1939.
  • Hisen Polymers & Chemical Laboratory (Edison, NJ): Specializing in custom synthetic rubbers, polymer shielding, and high-temperature electrical compounds.
  • Hisen Telecommunications Center (Sunnyvale, CA): Hub of our active fiber-optic switching, broadband systems research, and portal development operations.


Recent Press & Milestones (2002)
Hisen Corp Secures Broad-Scale Optical Cable Tender
Date: April 14th, 2002
Today, Hisen's Communications Board announced a landmark procurement contract to supply high-density optical fiber for metropolitan telecom backbones across three Midwestern states. CFO Arthur Hisen, Jr. remarked, "This represents a validation of our advanced polymer engineering. We remain the premier physical-layer choice."

Arthur Hisen, Jr. Honored at Century of Industry Expo
Date: June 22nd, 2002
At this year's Century of Industry Expo in Philadelphia, Hisen Corporation's leadership was recognized with the Lifetime Corporate Achievement Shield for our continuous supply of critical metal alloys during mid-century infrastructure booms.
